About Us
Welcome Co Ltd is sales office and joint partnership with TC Precision Casting Corporation, which was founded in 1985, is a primarily family owned manufacture, we dedicated to supplying our customers with highly engineered mechanical components with excellent service and quality. We honored with registered ISO9001-2000 and which was certified by United Kingdom UTKS United Kingdom.

Over the years, we have successfully grown due to the continued support from our customers and increase in demand for our products and services. Starting from a 38,000 square foot facility in 1985 delivering world-class mechanical components, we have grown today, with established our subsidiary facility in China in 1995, to two facilities. One facility is located in Taipei, Taiwan and our second facility and is located in Kunshan, China, now has over 200,000 square feet of manufacturing capacity and 230 employees to better serve you.

We have installed a CLA furnace since 2003 which is leading equipment and techniques that improved the accuracy and density.
