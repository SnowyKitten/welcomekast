Welcomekast uses a CLA(Counter-gravity low pressure air melt) furnance developed by US based company Hitchiner, that provides the ability to cast sections as thin as 0.015 of an inch. This is achieved by placing the mold inside a vacuum chamber where it is then lowered a precise distance into the melt, allowing accuracy for fine parts.
